Mashed Potato Recipe

Description

This quick and easy recipe for mashed potatoes is sure to fit all of your needs for the upcoming holiday season. When it comes to this tasty and beloved dish you want to be sure that you are making your family the best there is.

This recipe might not be very fancy, but it sure will get you exactly what it is that you came for, and will leave everybody wanting more, guaranteed! Get your peeler ready for this incredible mashed potato recipe.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds scrubbed, unpeeled potatoes
  • 1.5 cups half and half
  • Salt
  • 6 Tbsp Butter

Steps

  1. Place the potatoes in a large pan with water
  2. Bring water to boil, and leave potatoes covered until tender, 20-30 minutes
  3. Heat half and half in a microwave or over the stove
  4. Puree using a potato masher and stir in half and half with a little bit of salt
  5. Serve
