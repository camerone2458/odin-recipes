Quesadilla

Description

The Quesadilla is a timeless classic, which can be quickly made by anybody that is requiring an easy and simple meal. You can choose whether you want meat and cheese, just cheese, or some other tasty combination.

There are many different styles of cooking the quesadilla, with both raw and pre-cooked tortillas being used. The recipe below is a quick and easy way to make them using raw tortillas over the, and is my preferred way to make this wonderful food. It can serve as a snack or a meal, which is one of the most wonderful things about it.

Ingredients

  • Raw Tortilla
  • Cheese of Choice
  • Olive/Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Heat the stove to medium
  2. Oil a pan and place it over the burner
  3. Place the tortilla onto the pan
  4. Flip the tortilla once one side has become golden
  5. Place cheese on the golden side and fold in half.
  6. Once one side is golden flip to the final raw side
  7. When the final side is finished and the cheese is fully melted, remove from the pan
  8. Repeat starting at step 3 as many times as desired
