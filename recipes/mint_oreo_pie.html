Mint Oreo Pie Recipe

Description

This amazing dessert is sure to be a hit for you and your family, and is quick and easy enough to make that it can be done by anybody! Be sure to try it out this upcoming holiday season.

When you need to make a quick pie but don't have the time or the oven space to bake a pie crust, this quick and easy oreo recipe is exactly what you need. With only a few ingredients, and under 2 hours of time (most of which is spent waiting), this is the best recipe you can use!

Ingredients

  • 24 Oreos
  • 1/4 Cup Melted Butter
  • 1 Cup Andes Mints
  • 3/4 tsp Peppermint Flavoring
  • 1 1/4 Cup Heavy Cream
  • 3/4 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 8 oz Softened Cream Cheese
  • 1 1/4 Cup Powdered Sugar
  • Green Food Coloring

Steps

  1. Crush the oreos together with the butter
  2. Press crumb mixture into pie tin and refrigerate
  3. Mix Heavy Cream until Peaks form, move to bowl
  4. Add Cream cheese, beat for 2 minutes
  5. Add Powdered Sugar and food coling, mix until smooth
  6. Add Vanilla and Peppermint and mix until combined
  7. Fold Whipped cream into Cream cheese mixture with Andes Mints and mini chocolate chips(optional)
  8. Pour filling into Crust, sprinkle with Andes Mints
  9. Refrigerate until ready to eat
